Why This Wins

Two industries. Two dirty secrets.

The Supplement Problem

Most supplements are poorly absorbed. You're paying for 500mg on the label but your body might use 50. The rest is expensive urine. INSDR formulates for bioavailability — the amount your body actually takes in. Less waste. Real results.

The Activewear Problem

Most gym clothes are made from polyester and nylon — thermoplastics. The same material as plastic water bottles. These fabrics have tested positive for PFAs, forever chemicals linked to cancer and thyroid dysfunction. People are sweating in plastic pressed against their lymph nodes and calling it "performance wear."

INSDR says no to both.

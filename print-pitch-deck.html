The Foundation Stack

Three deficiencies. Three products.
Every ingredient chosen for absorption.

Hero SKU

Magnesium Complex

Albion TRAACS® chelated magnesium (body) + Magtein® threonate (brain) + B6 as P5P (cofactor).

Most people take oxide — 4% bioavailability. INSDR uses two forms with two delivery mechanisms. Customers feel it in 3-5 days (sleep). "Feel It First" 30-day guarantee.

Mito Complex

Kaneka QH® ubiquinol (~2x plasma CoQ10 vs. ubiquinone) + BioPQQ® (triggers new mitochondria) via VESIsorb® colloidal delivery.

622% higher absorption vs. standard softgel. CoQ10 levels drop after 25. Statin users are depleted. This is the product customers feel.

D3+K2 Complex

MenaQ7® K2 (99.8% all-trans purity — generics can be 15% active) + FruiteX-B® boron + liposomal D3.

4x higher absorption fasted vs. standard. No meal timing required. 68% of young Taiwanese adults are D-deficient.

Why these three: Deficiency-based, not trend-based. All daily-use (high subscription retention). Every product addresses both vitality and longevity. Universal across age groups — a 28-year-old's recovery stack is a 60-year-old's heart health stack. "The stack I put my mom on" is a purchase path.

Why Apparel Launches First

"Stop Sweating in Plastic" requires one sentence. Supplements require education. Cotton requires none.

Every garment in a gym is a zero-cost billboard. No supplement brand has physical presence in gyms. Apparel builds brand recognition so supplements launch into a warm audience 3-6 months later.

Simpler to manufacture. No regulatory review. No TFDA claims. No ingredient licensing. Lower risk, faster to market.
